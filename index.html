Gavin Cornwell

Founding Engineer & Software Architect

SMALL INTRODUCTION

Gavin has been a technical leader and software engineer for over 20 years, spending a majority of that in the Enterprise Content Management industry. Currently he's playing an architect role but over the last few years he has also been at the helm of the Content Services, Cloud and Mobile teams at Alfresco.

He also played an instrumental part in the concept, design and implementation of an Objective-C CMIS library that was ultimately contributed to the Apache Chemistry project where he remains an active participant and committer.

Being one of the founding engineers Gavin designed and implemented several key areas of the product in both the UI (mobile & web) and the application services layer.

Prior to Alfresco Gavin played a crucial role in the implementation of an innovative finance application using J2EE and .NET technologies and previously worked for Documentum both in the UK and the US, as a lead developer.
